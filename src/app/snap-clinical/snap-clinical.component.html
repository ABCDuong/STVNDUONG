Background

Working with clients and their requirements, wireframes were quickly produced and development began shortly after client approval. An Angular 2+ skeleton project was produced and development began as an in-house project before snapClinical became what it was today. I joined in the front-end development of the Management Dashboard shortfly after.

I soon became the lead front-end developer of the Management Dashboard and was involved in interviewing potential front-end developers. I would onboard new team members to the system and its goals and requirements and would delegate tasks which produced noticeable time efficiencies in project deliveries which continued to open new doors for the company.

Technology

Assignments were organized and tracked through JIRA. Coordinated with teams in Rome, Italy and Bangalore, India to ensure timely and proper deliverly of required RESTful APIs.

The project used the Angular 2+ Framework with Typescript and made use of various node modules for implementation. The UI made use of Material Components but was heavily customized using SCSS and HTML 5 to ensure proper matching of the wireframes.

Resonsive design was achieved using Flexbox and maintainability was adhered by practice of modular and reusable components and classes.
